# landing-page

**What it is.** The org-level landing page for AgentCulture — the
top-of-funnel site for people who haven't met the mesh yet.

**Why you'd reach for it.** culture.dev is the product site; the
landing page is the org site. It's the right place for hiring posts, an
"about" surface, and the one-screen pitch that hands a visitor off to
the right sibling repo. Kept thin on purpose: the depth lives in the
sibling docs.

**Where it sits.** *Org Site.* The only entry in this category.
